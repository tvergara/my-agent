# Bibliography Audit for Paper acca775c

**Paper ID:** acca775c-254b-410c-9252-c37ed998431f
**Title:** Expert Threshold Routing for Autoregressive Language Modeling with Dynamic Computation Allocation and Load Balancing

## Overview
I conducted a systematic audit of the bibliography (`example_paper.bib`) for this paper, focusing on citation accuracy, capitalization protection in BibTeX entries, and the currency of references.

## Findings

### 1. Missing Capitalization Protection for Proper Nouns and Acronyms
Several entries fail to protect acronyms and specific model names in their titles using curly braces. This can lead to incorrect lowercasing in many bibliography styles.

*   **Model Names and Acronyms:**
    *   `title={DataComp-LM: In Search of the Next Generation of Training Sets for Language Models}` -> Should be `{DataComp-LM}`.
    *   `title={OLMoE: Open Mixture-of-Experts Language Models}` -> Should be `{OLMoE}`.
    *   `title={Ada-K Routing: Boosting the Efficiency of MoE-based LLMs}` -> Should be `{Ada-K}`, `{MoE}`, and `{LLMs}`.
    *   `title={Mixture-of-Depths: Dynamically allocating compute in transformer-based language models}` -> `Mixture-of-Depths` should be protected.

### 2. Outdated arXiv Preprints
Several entries are cited as arXiv preprints even though they have been published in major peer-reviewed venues by now (April 2026).

*   **Raposo et al. (2024):** `title={Mixture-of-Depths: Dynamically allocating compute in transformer-based language models}`, `journal={arXiv preprint arXiv:2404.02258}`.
    *   **Status:** Published at **ICML 2024**.
*   **Li et al. (2024):** `title={DataComp-LM: In Search of the Next Generation of Training Sets for Language Models}`, `journal={arXiv preprint arXiv:2406.11794}`.
    *   **Status:** Published at **NeurIPS 2024**.
*   **Muennighoff et al. (2024):** `title={OLMoE: Open Mixture-of-Experts Language Models}`, `journal={arXiv preprint arXiv:2409.02060}`.
    *   **Status:** Likely published at a major conference (e.g., ICLR 2025 or similar) given its impact.

## Recommendations
*   Apply curly braces to all proper nouns and acronyms (MoE, LLM, OLMoE, DataComp-LM, etc.) in titles to ensure they are rendered correctly across all bibliography styles.
*   Update arXiv entries to reflect their final published versions in peer-reviewed venues like ICML, NeurIPS, and ICLR.
