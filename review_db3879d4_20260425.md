# Citation Formatting Audit for Paper db3879d4

## Paper Information
- **ID**: db3879d4-3184-4565-8ec8-7e30fb6312e6
- **Title**: Self-Supervised Flow Matching for Scalable Multi-Modal Synthesis

## Identified Issues

### 1. Missing Capitalization Protection in Bibliography Titles
A large number of entries in `example_paper.bib` contain technical acronyms, model names, and proper nouns that are not protected with curly braces `{}`. This will cause BibTeX to lowercase them in many styles (e.g., ICML style).

**Acronyms and Model Names:**
- `gans` should be `{GANs}` in `adm`, `mu_disc`, `is`, `projectedgan`, `stylegant`, `kang2023gigagan`, `kang2024distilling`.
- `BERT` should be `{BERT}` in `beit`, `bert`.
- `GPT` should be `{GPT}` in `openai2025gpt5`.
- `VAE` should be `{VAE}` in `repae`, `evvae`, `eqvae`.
- `DINOv2` should be `{DINOv2}` in `dinov2`.
- `Imagenet` should be `{ImageNet}` in `imgnet`.
- `Adam` should be `{Adam}` in `adam`.
- `DiT` should be `{DiT}` in `chen2025ditairrevisitingefficiencydiffusion`.
- `DART` should be `{DART}` in `dart`.
- `MDT` should be `{MDT}` in `mdt`, `mdtv2`.
- `REPA` should be `{REPA}` in `repa`, `wang2025repa`.

**Technical Terms often requiring capitalization or protection:**
- `transformers` / `transformer` in `chen2021empirical`, `sit`, `dit`, `zhu2024sd`, `maskgit`, `vqgan`, `vit`, `sd3`, `pixart-alpha`, `vdt`, `shazeer2020glu`, `su2024roformer`, `rt1`.
- `diffusion` in `sit`, `dit`, `ldm`, `cfg`, `cfg_interval`, `zhu2024sd`, `edm`, `ddpm`, `ddim`, `pixart-alpha`, `vdt`, `xiang_disc`, `li_disc`, `neuraldm`, `fuest2024diffusion`, `stylegan3t`, `sauer2024`, `uvit`, `soda`, `abstreiter2021`, `li2024aligning`, `kang2024distilling`, `ddt`, `pixnerd`, `chen2024deconstructing`, `Chen2025MaskedAA`, `chen2024diffusion`, `song2021denoising`, `chen2023importance`, `Hoogeboom2023simpleDE`, `rombach2022highresolutionimagesynthesislatent`, `kingma2023understandingdiffusionobjectiveselbo`, `falck2025fourierspaceperspectivediffusion`, `black2023zeroshotroboticmanipulationpretrained`.

### 2. Typo/Inconsistent Casing
- `Imagenet` (lowercase 'n') in `imgnet` title: "Imagenet: A large-scale hierarchical image database".
- `gans` (lowercase) in multiple titles.
- `transformer` (lowercase) in multiple titles.
