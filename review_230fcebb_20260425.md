# Citation and Bibliography Audit: Why Depth Matters in Parallelizable Sequence Models: A Lie Algebraic View

I have performed a systematic audit of the bibliography (`main.bib` and `main_2.bib`) and in-text citations for the submission "Why Depth Matters in Parallelizable Sequence Models: A Lie Algebraic View" (ID: 230fcebb). My review focused on capitalization protection, reference currency, and formatting consistency.

## Summary of Findings

1. **Missing Capitalization Protection in BibTeX**: Many entries in the `main.bib` file lack curly brace protection `{}` for proper nouns, acronyms, and specific mathematical terms in their titles. This results in incorrect lowercasing in the final bibliography.
   * **Mathematical Terms/Names**: `Lie` (multiple entries), `Magnus` (multiple entries), `Krohn-Rhodes` (Maler 2010), `Kaluzhnin-Krasner` (Petrogradsky et al. 2007; Deval et al. 2024), `Lyndon` (multiple entries), `Hopf` (Underwood 2011), `Banach-Caccioppoli`, `Freedman`.
   * **Model/System Names**: `Kimi`, `RWKV-7`, `Goose`.
   * **Acronyms**: `IV` (Chen et al. 1958), `DAG` (Cheng et al. 2025).

2. **Outdated Reference Metadata**: Several works are cited as arXiv preprints despite having been formally published in major venues:
   * **Log neural controlled differential equations (Walker et al. 2024)**: Currently cited as `arXiv:2402.18512`. It was published in **ICML 2024**.
   * **On limitation of transformer for learning HMMs (Hu et al. 2024)**: Currently cited as `arXiv:2406.04089`. It was published in **COLM 2024**.

3. **Inconsistent Venue Naming**:
   * There is a mixture of using full conference names and macros/abbreviations. For example, `morrill2021neural` uses the full string `International Conference on Machine Learning`, while `merrill2024illusion` uses the macro `confICML` (which expands to `Proc. Int. Conf. on Machine Learning (ICML)`).
   * `sieber2024understanding` uses the full name `Advances in Neural Information Processing Systems`, while other entries use `confNIPS` or `confNEU`.

4. **BibTeX Typos**:
   * In `walker2024log`, the title contains `lie brackets` which should be capitalized as `{Lie} brackets`.
   * Duplicate `@STRING` definitions for `PAMI`, `ACL`, etc., at the beginning of `main.bib`.

## Recommendations
* Apply `{}` protection to all proper nouns (Lie, Magnus, etc.) and acronyms in `main.bib`.
* Update the bibliography to reflect formal publications for the 2024 works cited as preprints.
* Standardize `booktitle` fields to use a consistent format (either all macros or all full names).
