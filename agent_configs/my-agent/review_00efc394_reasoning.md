# Bibliography Audit Reasoning - Paper 00efc394

Paper Title: PersonalToken: Contrastive Personalization of Text-to-Image Generation via Personal Token Inversion
Paper ID: 00efc394-46c5-430c-8433-871d37d80092

## Audit Findings

### 1. Missing Capitalization Protection in Titles
Widespread lack of curly brace `{}` protection for acronyms, model names, and proper nouns. Without this, these terms will be incorrectly lowercased in many bibliography styles:
- **Models/Systems**: `{BERTScore}`, `{BERT}`, `{LLM}`, `{LLM}s`, `{GPT}-4`, `Gi{LOT}`, `{R}ho-1`, `{Q}wen2`, `{P}hi-1.5`, `{A}tari`.
- **Organizations/Venues**: `{ACM SIGSAC}`, `{IEEE}`, `{CSDN}`.
- **Acronyms**: `{NLG}`, `{CNN}s`, `{MCP}`, `{API}`.

### 2. Messy or Autogenerated BibTeX Keys
Some entries have extremely long or autogenerated-style keys that should be cleaned for better maintainability:
- `25TiaoXiaoXiDuoZhiNengTi_RuGuoWoBianChengHuiYilDeBoKeCSDNBoKe`: Appears to be an autogenerated key from a CSDN blog post, containing non-standard characters and excessive length.

### 3. Outdated arXiv Citations
Several entries cite arXiv preprints that have since been formally published:
- `haHyperNetworks2016` (arXiv:1609.09106) was published in **ICLR 2017**.
- `gur2023real` (arXiv:2307.12856) has likely published versions.
- `liu2023g` (arXiv:2303.16634) is a widely cited work (G-Eval) and should be updated to its definitive version.

### 4. Redundant or Incomplete Metadata
- **Truncated Author Lists**: `bommasaniOpportunitiesRisksFoundation2022` and `lin2024rho` use `others` in the BibTeX source, which is acceptable but inconsistent with other entries that list a large number of authors.

## Recommendation
Standardize title capitalization using curly braces, clean up autogenerated BibTeX keys, and update preprints to their definitive published versions.
