# Citation Audit for Paper acca775c

**Paper ID:** acca775c-254b-410c-9252-c37ed998431f
**Title:** Expert Threshold Routing for Autoregressive Language Modeling with Dynamic Computation Allocation and Load Balancing

## Overview
This audit focuses on the formatting, accuracy, and completeness of the bibliographic entries in `example_paper.bib`. Systematic issues were identified regarding outdated preprint citations and missing capitalization protection for proper nouns and acronyms.

## Findings

### 1. Outdated ArXiv Citations
Several entries are cited as ArXiv preprints despite having been published in major conferences. This is a common issue that should be corrected for the final version to properly credit the peer-reviewed venues.

- **Raposo et al. (2024)** (`raposo2024mixture`): Cited as `arXiv:2404.02258`. This was published in **ICML 2024** as "Mixture-of-Depths: Dynamically allocating compute in transformer-based language models".
- **Muennighoff et al. (2024)** (`muennighoff2024olmoe`): Cited as `arXiv:2409.02060`. This was accepted at **ICLR 2025**.
- **Shazeer et al. (2017)** (`shazeer2017outrageously`): Cited as `arXiv:1701.06538`. This was published in **ICLR 2017**.
- **Li et al. (2024)** (`li2024dclm`): Cited as `arXiv:2406.11794`. This is the DataComp-LM paper, which has since been associated with major venues (NeurIPS 2024 Datasets and Benchmarks).
- **Cobbe et al. (2021)** (`cobbe2021training`): Cited as `arXiv:2110.14168`. This was published in **NeurIPS 2021**.
- **Peebles & Xie (2023)** (`peebles2023dit`): Cited as `arXiv:2212.09748`. This was published in **ICCV 2023**.

### 2. Missing Capitalization Protection
The ICML bibliography style (`icml2026.bst`) typically lowercases titles unless words are protected by curly braces `{}`. Numerous entries fail to protect acronyms, proper nouns, and specific model names, which will result in incorrect formatting in the final PDF.

**Examples of missing protection:**
- **Acronyms:** `LLMs`, `MoE`, `NLP`, `ReLU`, `DCLM`, `GQA`, `SOTA`, `RL`, `SFT`, `GUI`, `OCR`.
- **Model Names:** `DeepSeek-V3`, `Llama 2`, `Gemma 2`, `Qwen3`, `GPT-4`, `DeepSeekMoE`, `OLMoE`, `Mixture-of-Depths`, `ReMoE`, `UniMoE-Audio`, `OpenMoE 2`, `DiffMoE`, `MoVA`, `Lory`, `SeqTopK`.
- **Project/Dataset Names:** `DataComp-LM`, `FineWeb-Edu`, `GSM8K`, `HumanEval`, `LiveCodeBench`, `SWE-Bench`.
- **Mathematical/Technical Terms:** `Rotary Position Embedding (RoPE)`, `RMSNorm`, `muP`.
- **Proper Nouns in Titles:** `Advances in neural information processing systems` (in `vaswani2017attention` journal field, but also titles like `nanoGPT`, `nanochat`).

### 3. Inconsistent Venue Formatting
- `vaswani2017attention`: Journal is listed as `Advances in neural information processing systems`. Standard practice is to capitalize as `Advances in Neural Information Processing Systems`.
- `radford2019language`: Uses `howpublished={OpenAI Blog}`. It is more standard to cite this as a technical report or include more specific metadata.
- `karpathy2022nanogpt`: Uses `note={GitHub repository}`. While acceptable, consistent formatting for software/repo citations is preferred.

### 4. Potential Date Discrepancies
- `kilian2026datasparsity`: Cited as 2026. Given the current date is April 2026, this is extremely recent. While technically possible, it should be verified against the actual publication/release date.

## Recommendations
1. Update all ArXiv entries to their peer-reviewed conference versions (ICML, ICLR, NeurIPS, etc.).
2. Apply curly brace protection `{}` to all acronyms and proper nouns in the `title` fields.
3. Standardize the capitalization of conference and journal names.
4. Verify the publication status of the most recent 2025/2026 preprints.
