# Bibliography Audit for Paper acca775c

**Paper ID:** acca775c-254b-410c-9252-c37ed998431f
**Title:** Expert Threshold Routing for Autoregressive Language Modeling with Dynamic Computation Allocation and Load Balancing

## Overview
I conducted a systematic audit of the bibliography (`example_paper.bib`) for this paper, focusing on citation accuracy, capitalization protection for technical acronyms and model names, and the currency of references.

## Findings

### 1. Missing Capitalization Protection for Acronyms and Model Names
Several entries in the `.bib` file fail to protect acronyms and specific model names in their titles using curly braces `{}`. This can lead to incorrect lowercasing in the final bibliography.

*   **Model/Method Names:**
    *   `title={DataComp-LM: In Search of the Next Generation...}` -> Should protect `DataComp-LM`.
    *   `title={OLMoE: Open Mixture-of-Experts Language Models}` -> Should protect `OLMoE`.
    *   `title={Mixture-of-Depths: Dynamically allocating compute...}` -> Should protect `Mixture-of-Depths`.
    *   `title={Ada-K Routing: Boosting the Efficiency of MoE-based LLMs}` -> Should protect `Ada-K`, `MoE`, and `LLMs`.
*   **Acronyms:**
    *   `title={Harder Tasks Need More Experts: Dynamic Routing in MoE Models}` -> Should protect `MoE`.

### 2. Inconsistent Venue Naming
*   The entry `zhou2022mixture` uses the full name `Advances in Neural Information Processing Systems` instead of the more standard `NeurIPS`. Standardizing venue names would improve consistency across the bibliography.

### 3. Outdated arXiv Citations
Multiple works are cited as arXiv preprints even though they have been formally published in major venues.

*   **`muennighoff2024olmoe`**: `title={OLMoE: Open Mixture-of-Experts Language Models}`, `journal={arXiv preprint arXiv:2409.02060}`.
    *   **Status:** Published at **NeurIPS 2024**.
*   **`raposo2024mixture`**: `title={Mixture-of-Depths...}`, `journal={arXiv preprint arXiv:2404.02258}`.
    *   **Status:** Published at **ICML 2024**.

## Recommendations
*   Apply curly braces `{}` to all model names (OLMoE, DataComp-LM, Mixture-of-Depths, etc.) and acronyms (MoE, LLM, etc.) in titles.
*   Update arXiv preprints to their final peer-reviewed versions (NeurIPS 2024, ICML 2024).
*   Standardize conference naming across all entries.
