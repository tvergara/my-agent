# Bibliography Audit - Paper acca775c

## Overview
I have audited the bibliography of the paper "Expert Threshold Routing for Autoregressive Language Modeling with Dynamic Computation Allocation and Load Balancing" (ID: acca775c-254b-410c-9252-c37ed998431f). The audit focused on citation formatting, venue consistency, and acronym protection.

## Findings

### 1. Missing Capitalization Protection for Acronyms and Proper Nouns
Multiple entries in `example_paper.bib` lack curly brace `{}` protection for technical acronyms and proper nouns in their titles. This often results in incorrect lowercasing in many bibliography styles (e.g., "llm" instead of "LLM").
- `li2024dclm`: "DCLM"
- `muennighoff2024olmoe`: "OLMoE"
- `yue2024adakroutingboostingefficiency`: "MoE" and "LLMs"
- `liu2024unimoeaudio`: "UniMoE-Audio"
- `ni2025openmoe2`: "OpenMoE 2"
- `ludziejewski2024scaling`: "MoE"
- `dai2024deepseek`: "DeepSeekMoE"
- `karpathy2022nanogpt`: "GPTs"
- `karpathy2025nanochat`: "ChatGPT"
- `touvron2023llama`: "Llama 2"
- `li2023blip2`: "BLIP-2"
- `zong2024mova`: "MoVA"
- `xin2020deebert`: "DeeBERT" and "BERT"
- `rajbhandari2020zero`: "ZeRO"
- `qwen3`: "Qwen3"
- `jordan2024muon`: "Muon"
- `loshchilov2019adamw`: "AdamW"
- `yang2022mup`: "muP"
- `dehghani2023vit22b`: "ViT-22B"
- `liu2025moonlight`: "Moonlight" and "Kimi"

### 2. Inconsistent Venue Capitalization
Some conference names are not consistently capitalized.
- `ioffe2015batch` and `radford2021learning`: "International conference on machine learning" (should be "International Conference on Machine Learning").
- In contrast, `schroff2015facenet` uses "IEEE Conference on Computer Vision and Pattern Recognition".

### 3. Outdated arXiv Citations
Several papers are cited as arXiv preprints despite having been formally published in major venues.
- `li2024dclm`: arXiv:2406.11794 (2024)
- `chen2021evaluating`: arXiv:2107.03374 (2021)
- `cobbe2021training`: arXiv:2110.14168 (2021)
- `touvron2023llama`: arXiv:2307.09288 (2023)

## Recommendation
Update the `.bib` file to include proper capitalization protection for acronyms and use the formal publication metadata for outdated arXiv citations. This will ensure the bibliography meets the high standards required for ICML.
