# Bibliographic Audit - Paper acca775c

## Overview
This audit focuses on the bibliographic consistency and accuracy of the paper "Expert Threshold Routing for Autoregressive Language Modeling with Dynamic Computation Allocation and Load Balancing" (acca775c).

## Findings

### 1. Outdated arXiv Citations
Several entries are cited as arXiv preprints despite having been published in major peer-reviewed conferences:
- **li2024dclm**: Cited as `arXiv preprint arXiv:2406.11794` (2024). It was published in **NeurIPS 2024** (Datasets and Benchmarks Track).
- **raposo2024mixture**: Cited as `arXiv preprint arXiv:2404.02258` (2024). It was published in **ICML 2024**.
- **dai2024deepseek**: Cited as `arXiv preprint arXiv:2401.06066` (2024). It was published in **ACL 2024**.
- **muennighoff2024olmoe**: Cited as `arXiv preprint arXiv:2409.02060` (2024). It was published in **ICLR 2025**.
- **shazeer2017outrageously**: Cited as `arXiv preprint arXiv:1701.06538` (2017). It was published in **ICLR 2017**.

### 2. Missing Capitalization Protection
The following titles contain proper nouns or technical terms that require protection with curly braces `{}` to prevent them from being lowercased by certain bibliography styles (e.g., those using sentence case):
- **li2024dclm**: `DataComp-LM` should be `{DataComp-LM}`.
- **touvron2023llama**: `Llama 2` should be `{Llama 2}`.
- **muennighoff2024olmoe**: `OLMoE` should be `{OLMoE}`.
- **raposo2024mixture**: `transformer-based` should be `{Transformer}-based`.
- **dai2024deepseek**: `DeepSeekMoE` should be `{DeepSeekMoE}`.
- **deepseekv3**: `DeepSeek-V3` should be `{DeepSeek-V3}`.
- **sun2024ecdit**: `EC-DIT` should be `{EC-DIT}`.
- **shi2025diffmoe**: `DiffMoE` should be `{DiffMoE}`.
- **jin2024moe++**: `MoE++` should be `{MoE++}`.
- **kilian2026datasparsity**: `MoE` should be `{MoE}`.
- **qwen3**: `Qwen3` should be `{Qwen3}`.
- **longcat2025**: `LongCat-Flash` should be `{LongCat-Flash}`.
- **zong2024mova**: `MoVA` should be `{MoVA}`.
- **lin2024moma**: `MoMa` should be `{MoMa}`.
- **luo2024gamma**: `MoD` should be `{MoD}`.

### 3. Inconsistencies in Technical Reports
- **deepseekv3**, **qwen3**, and **longcat2025** are technical reports but use the `@misc` type with `eprint`. While acceptable, they should ideally include the institution or team explicitly in the `publisher` or `institution` field if available.

## Conclusion
Updating these entries will significantly improve the scholarly quality of the manuscript and ensure that the most recent and authoritative versions of the cited works are referenced.
