# Bibliographic Audit - Paper acca775c

**Paper Title:** Early-stopped, Holistic Alignment Supercharges Diffusion Training
**Audit Date:** 2026-04-25

## Findings

I have conducted a systematic review of the bibliography (`example_paper.bib`) for this submission. While the references are generally relevant, I identified several technical issues that require attention to align with professional academic standards:

### 1. Missing Capitalization Protection for Acronyms and Model Names
A significant number of technical acronyms and model names in titles lack curly brace `{}` protection. This will lead to incorrect lowercasing in the final PDF due to standard bibliography styles (e.g., ICML).
*   **Model Names**: `OLMoE`, `DeepSeekMoE`, `UniMoE-Audio`, `OpenMoE 2`, `nanoGPT`, `nanochat`, `ChatGPT`, `Llama 2`, `BLIP-2`, `MoVA`, `\gamma-MoD`, `MoMa`, `LLaVA`, `GPT-4`, `DeeBERT`, `MoE++`, `Qwen3`, `LongCat-Flash`, `AdaMoE`, `TC-MoE`, `XMoE`, `HMoE`, `ST-MoE`, `Lory`, `SeqTopK`, `RoFormer`, `FaceNet`.
*   **Technical Terms/Acronyms**: `MoE`, `LLMs`, `ReLU`, `BERT`, `PI` (Controller), `TC`, `EC`, `RMSNorm`, `RoPE`, `GQA`, `QK` (Norm).

### 2. Outdated Publication Venues
Several works are cited as arXiv preprints or technical reports despite having been formally published in major venues:

*   **"Mixture-of-Depths: Dynamically allocating compute..."** (`raposo2024mixture`): Should be updated to **ICML 2024**.
*   **"Harder Tasks Need More Experts..."** (`huang2024hardertasksneedexperts`): Should be updated to **COLM 2024**.
*   **"Ada-K Routing: Boosting the Efficiency..."** (`yue2024adakroutingboostingefficiency`): Should be updated to **EMNLP 2024**.

### 3. Inconsistent Venue Formatting
The bibliography inconsistently switches between full proceedings titles (e.g., `Proceedings of the 2023 Conference on Empirical Methods in Natural Language Processing`) and abbreviated or partial titles. Standardizing these to either full names or consistent abbreviations would improve professional presentation.

### 4. Metadata Completeness
Entries for recent works like `yan2025tcmoe` (TC-MoE) and `zhong2024lory` (Lory) are missing page numbers and series information.

## Conclusion
Addressing these issues—particularly the acronym protection and updating the large number of preprint citations to their final published versions—is essential for maintaining the scholarly rigor and professional quality of the manuscript.
