# Citation Formatting Audit for Paper acca775c

## Overview
This audit focuses on the formatting of the bibliography for the paper "Expert Threshold Routing for Autoregressive Language Modeling with Dynamic Computation Allocation and Load Balancing" (ID: `acca775c`).

## Methodology
The LaTeX source and BibTeX files were extracted from the provided tarball. The `icml2026.bst` file was inspected to determine how titles are processed. It was confirmed that the bibliography style uses sentence case conversion (`change.case$ {t}`), which lowercases any unbraced capital letters in titles.

A systematic check was performed on `example_paper.bib` to identify unbraced acronyms and proper names in titles.

## Findings
The audit identified 25 entries in `example_paper.bib` where technical acronyms or proper names in the title are not protected by braces `{}`. Under the ICML 2026 bibliography style, these will be rendered incorrectly (e.g., "LLM" will become "llm", "MoE" will become "moe").

### Key Examples:
- **li2024dclm**: "DataComp-LM" is unprotected.
- **muennighoff2024olmoe**: "OLMoE" is unprotected.
- **yue2024adakroutingboostingefficiency**: "MoE" and "LLMs" are unprotected.
- **wang2025remoe**: "ReMoE" is unprotected.
- **liu2024unimoeaudio**: "UniMoE-Audio" and "MoE" are unprotected.
- **deepseekv3**: "DeepSeek-V3" is unprotected.
- **touvron2023llama**: "Llama" is unprotected.
- **luo2024gamma**: "MoD" is unprotected.
- **lin2024moma**: "MoMa" is unprotected.
- **openai2023gpt4**: "GPT-4" is unprotected.

## Conclusion
The bibliography requires significant corrections to preserve the capitalization of technical terms. Protecting these terms with braces is necessary for a professional and correct camera-ready submission.
