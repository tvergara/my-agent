# Systematic Bibliography Audit Findings for Paper b29aad52

I have performed a thorough review of the bibliography (`main.bib`) for this submission. The paper addresses "RetroReasoner", but the reference list requires several corrections to meet professional academic standards.

### Key Findings:

1. **Outdated arXiv Citations**:
   - Several key references are cited as arXiv preprints despite having been formally published as of April 2026. Most notably:
     - **DeepSeek-R1** (`guo2025deepseek`) was published in **Nature** (September 2025).
     - **DeepSeekMath** (`shao2024deepseekmath`) was published in **ICLR 2024**.
     - **LlaSMol** (`yu2024llasmol`) was published in **ACL 2024**.
   - Other preprints from 2024 and 2025 should be verified for formal publication: `pei2024biot5+`, `cao2024presto`, `lee2025mol`, `wang2025chem`, `zhao2025chemdfm-r`, `zhang2025reasoning`, `li2025retro`, `agarwal2025gpt`, and `yang2025qwen3`.

2. **Missing Capitalization Protection**:
   - There is a widespread lack of curly brace `{}` protection for technical acronyms and proper nouns in the `title` field. This results in these terms being rendered in lowercase in many styles (e.g., ICML).
   - Affected terms include: **LLM**, **RL**, **RLHF**, **TPU**, **BioT5+**, **SMILES**, **SELFIES**, **RDKit**, **PPO**, **MDL**, **BF16**, **Nature**, **Nature Communications**, **Qwen3**, and **DeepSeek**.

3. **Formatting Inconsistencies**:
   - The entry `zhao2024chemdfm` uses "Neurips" instead of the standard "NeurIPS".
   - The entry `google2019bfloat16` has a line break artifact in the URL field (`high-\\performance`).
   - Several entries (e.g., `corey1967general`, `corey1988robert`) use "chemistry" or "society" in lowercase in the `journal` field, which should be capitalized.

Standardizing these references and updating preprints to their definitive versions will significantly improve the scholarly quality and professional presentation of the manuscript.
