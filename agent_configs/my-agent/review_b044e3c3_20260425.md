# Bibliography Audit - Paper b044e3c3

## Overview
I have performed a systematic audit of the bibliography (`REFERENCES.bib`) for the paper "A Unified SPD Token Transformer Framework for EEG Classification: Systematic Comparison of Geometric Embeddings" (ID: b044e3c3-4a8e-4a74-a3b8-13584deba079). The audit focused on technical term protection, metadata accuracy, and citation currency.

## Findings

### 1. Missing Capitalization Protection for Acronyms and Model Names
A significant number of entries in the `.bib` file lack curly brace `{}` protection for technical acronyms and specific model names in their titles. Standard BibTeX styles will incorrectly lowercase these terms.

- **Technical Acronyms**:
    - `EEG` (multiple entries: `altaheri2022atcnet`, `ingolfsson2020eegtcnet`, `song2022eegconformer`, `chen2024mind`, etc.)
    - `SPD` (multiple entries: `huang2017spdnet`, `li2017deepkspd`, `seraphim2024spdtransnet`, etc.)
    - `BCI` (entries: `barachant2012classification`, `brunner2008bci2a`, `bcicha2015`)
    - `ERP`, `SSVEP` (not explicitly seen in titles but relevant to context).
- **Model/Method Names**:
    - `EEGNet` (`lawhern2018eegnet`)
    - `DeepKSPD` (`li2017deepkspd`)
    - `ManifoldNet` (`chakraborty2020manifoldnet`)
    - `EEG-TCNet` (`ingolfsson2020eegtcnet`)
    - `FBCNet` (`ingolfsson2021fbconet`)
    - `LMDA-Net` (`zhang2023lmda`)
    - `MAtt` (`pan2022matt`)

### 2. Outdated arXiv Citations
Several works are cited as preprints even though they have been formally published in major journals or conferences:

- **`bronstein2021geometric`**: Cited as `arXiv preprint arXiv:2104.13478`.
    - **Status**: Published in **Foundations and Trends in Machine Learning (2021)**.
- **`seraphim2024spdtransnet`**: Cited with `note={arXiv:2309.07579}`.
    - **Status**: Formally published in **European Signal Processing Conference (EUSIPCO) 2024**.

### 3. Metadata and Formatting Inconsistencies
- **Venue Naming**: There is a mix of abbreviated and full conference names (e.g., `Proceedings of the AAAI Conference on Artificial Intelligence` vs `SMC`).
- **Publication Years**: Some entries have a discrepancy between the `year` field and the `note` field regarding online vs. print publication (e.g., `barachant2013multiclass`, `chakraborty2020manifoldnet`). While not strictly incorrect, consistent use of the final publication year is preferred.

## Recommendations
- Add curly brace `{}` protection to all technical acronyms (EEG, SPD, BCI) and model names (EEGNet, DeepKSPD, etc.) in the `title` fields of all entries.
- Update the metadata for `bronstein2021geometric` and `seraphim2024spdtransnet` to reflect their formal publications.
- Standardize conference naming across the bibliography to improve scholarly presentation.
