# Bibliographic Audit - Paper c993ba35

## Paper Information
- **ID**: c993ba35-65e0-4290-a66a-c128e33410f4
- **Title**: Learning Approximate Nash Equilibria in Cooperative Multi-Agent Reinforcement Learning via Mean-Field Subsampling
- **Audit Date**: 2026-04-25

## Summary of Findings
The bibliography (`main.bib`) for this submission contains numerous technical acronyms in titles that lack capitalization protection. Furthermore, there are significant inconsistencies in how major conference venues (ICML, NeurIPS, AISTATS) are named across different entries.

## Detailed Findings

### 1. Unprotected Acronyms in Titles
Acronyms in the `title` field must be wrapped in curly braces `{}` to prevent BibTeX from converting them to lowercase. Many entries fail this requirement.

**Examples identified:**
- `TD-learning` and `TD` in `chen2021finite`, `chen2021lyapunov`, `9570295`
- `Q-learning` and `Q` in `chen2021lyapunov`, `9570295`, `yang2019sample`, `doi:10.1137/20M1360700`, `gu2021meanfieldcontrolsqlearningcooperative`
- `MARL` in `8807386`, `10.5555/3586589.3586718`, `doi:10.1137/20M1360700`, `Hu_Wei_Yan_Zhang_2023`, `gu2021meanfieldcontrolsqlearningcooperative`
- `AI` in `overman2025oversightgamelearningcooperatively`, `yang2025agentexchangeshapingfuture`
- `MDP` in `deweese2024locally`
- `UAV` in `8807386`, `Yang_2023`
- `POMDPs` in `10.5555/3600270.3600377`
- `Nash` (Proper name) in `doi:10.1073/pnas.36.1.48`, `daskalakis2009complexity`
- `Stackelberg` (Proper name) in `balcan2025learningstructuredstackelberggames`, `bichuch2024stackelberg`, `10.5555/3540261.3542236`, `10.5555/3618408.3618858`, `bruckner2011stackelberg`, `gan2025robust`, `yu2022learningcorrelatedstackelbergequilibrium`, `4597505`, `10310098`, `bacchiocchi2024samplecomplexitystackelberggames`

### 2. Inconsistent Venue Formatting
The bibliography entries for the same conferences use widely different naming conventions:
- **ICML**:
    - `International Conference on Machine Learning`
    - `Forty-first International Conference on Machine Learning`
    - `Proceedings of the Nineteenth International Conference on Machine Learning (ICML 2002)`
    - `Proceedings of the 40th International Conference on Machine Learning`
- **NeurIPS**:
    - `Advances in Neural Information Processing Systems`
    - `The Thirty-ninth Annual Conference on Neural Information Processing Systems`
- **AISTATS**:
    - `Proceedings of The 25th International Conference on Artificial Intelligence and Statistics`
    - `International Conference on Artificial Intelligence and Statistics`

### 3. arXiv Consistency
There are variations in the `journal` field for preprints:
- `arXiv preprint arXiv:2210.12320`
- `ArXiv` (with volume `abs/1610.03295`)
- `arXiv preprint arXiv: 2504.15466` (extraneous space after colon)

## Recommendations
- Use a consistent naming convention for all major conferences (e.g., always include the edition number or always omit it).
- Systematically check all titles for acronyms and proper names (e.g., `Nash`, `Stackelberg`, `Markov`) and wrap them in braces.
- Standardize the `journal` field for preprints to `arXiv preprint arXiv:XXXX.XXXXX`.
- Consolidation of publisher and organization fields (e.g., `PMLR` vs `Curran Associates, Inc.`).
