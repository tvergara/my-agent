# Citation Audit for Paper c993ba35

**Paper ID:** c993ba35-65e0-4290-a66a-c128e33410f4
**Title:** Learning Approximate Nash Equilibria in Cooperative Multi-Agent Reinforcement Learning via Mean-Field Subsampling

## Overview
This audit examines the bibliographic quality of `main.bib` for the paper "Learning Approximate Nash Equilibria". The analysis focuses on capitalization consistency, venue accuracy, and the status of preprint citations.

## Findings

### 1. Inconsistent Capitalization Protection
The bibliography uses a mix of specific brace protection and global title protection (double brackets `{{...}}`). While double-bracketing is a common shortcut to preserve casing, its inconsistent application makes the bibliography feel unpolished.

- **Double-bracketed (Protected):** `chen2021finite`, `chen2021lyapunov`, `lv2024localinformationaggregationbased`, `8807386`, `deweese2024locally`, `anand2025meanfield`, `anand2026graphonmeanfieldsubsamplingcooperative`.
- **Partially Protected:** ` Silver_Huang_Maddison_Guez_Sifre_van_den_Driessche_Schrittwieser_Antonoglou_Panneershelvam_Lanctot_et_al._2016` (Mastering the {G}ame of {G}o).
- **Unprotected (Risk of lowercasing):** `lin2022online` (No-Regret via Contractive Perturbations), `lazarsfeld2025fastfurioussymmetriclearning` (Zero-Sum Games: Gradient Descent as Fictitious Play).

### 2. Outdated ArXiv Citations
Several papers are cited as preprints but have been published in formal venues or have more recent versions:
- **Lin et al. (2022)** (`lin2022online`): Cited as `arXiv:2210.12320`.
- **Lazarsfeld et al. (2025)** (`lazarsfeld2025fastfurioussymmetriclearning`): Cited as `arXiv:2506.13086`.
- **Guo et al. (2025)** (`guo2025markovalphapotentialgames`): Cited as `arXiv:2305.12553`.
- **Wainwright (2019)** (`wainwright2019variance`): Cited as `arXiv:1906.04697`. This is widely cited from **JMLR** or as a published work.

### 3. Inconsistent Entry Types and Formatting
- `anand2023pseudorandomness`: Listed as `@article` with `journal={Caltech Undergraduate Thesis}`. This is more accurately cited as a `@thesis` or `@techreport`.
- `bichuch2024stackelberg`: Listed as `@article` with `journal={Available at SSRN 4985557}`.
- `NAAMAN2021109088`: Includes full ISSN and URL, which are often omitted in conference bibliographies to save space.

### 4. Recent Citations (2025/2026)
The bibliography contains many citations from 2025 and 2026 (e.g., `anand2026graphonmeanfieldsubsamplingcooperative`, `wang2026unsuperviseddecompositionrecombinationdiscriminatordriven`). Given the current date is April 2026, these are appropriate but should be checked for updated venue information (e.g., if a 2026 preprint was accepted to a 2026 conference).

## Recommendations
1. Standardize capitalization protection across all entries.
2. Update ArXiv preprints to their corresponding conference/journal versions.
3. Clean up inconsistent entry types (`@thesis` vs `@article`).
4. Ensure all acronyms (`AI`, `MARL`, `MDP`, `Nash`) are protected in unprotected titles.
