# Bibliography Audit - Paper db3879d4

## Paper Information
- **Title**: Self-Supervised Flow Matching for Scalable Multi-Modal Synthesis
- **ID**: db3879d4-3184-4565-8ec8-7e30fb6312e6

## Audit Findings

### 1. Outdated arXiv Citations
Several foundational methods are cited as arXiv preprints despite being published in major venues:

- `adam`: Cited as arXiv 2014. Should be updated to **ICLR 2015**.
- `adamw`: Cited as arXiv 2017. Should be updated to **ICLR 2019**.
- `resnet`: Often cited as CVPR 2016.
- `vit`: Often cited as ICLR 2021.

### 2. Missing Capitalization Protection (BibTeX)
There is a lack of `{}` protection for technical acronyms and system names in the titles, which will result in incorrect rendering in many styles.

Examples:
- **Sd-DiT**, **Diffusion Transformer**, **SongBloom**, **Wan**, **VAE**, **GAN**, **ViT**, **ResNet**, **CLIP**, **DINOv2**, **MAE**, **FMA**.

### 3. Informal Citation Sources
- The bibliography contains several entries pointing to social media ("X Post on Representation Alignment"). While sometimes necessary for the absolute latest discussion, formal academic citations or more permanent archival sources (like technical reports) are preferred where available.

### 4. Typographical Consistency
- `zhu2024sd`: The title uses "Sd-dit" and "diffusion transformer" without proper capitalization or protection. It should be corrected to `{{S}d-{D}i{T}}` and `{D}iffusion {T}ransformer`.

## Conclusion
The bibliography requires minor updates to reflect formal publication venues for foundational optimization and architecture papers, along with systematic case protection for technical acronyms.
