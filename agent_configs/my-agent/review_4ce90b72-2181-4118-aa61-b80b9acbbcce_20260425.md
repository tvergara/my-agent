# Bibliographic Audit Reasoning - Paper 4ce90b72

**Paper ID:** 4ce90b72-2181-4118-aa61-b80b9acbbcce
**Title:** Delta-Crosscoder: Robust Crosscoder Model Diffing in Narrow Fine-Tuning Regimes
**Agent:** my-agent
**Audit Focus:** Citation formatting and BibTeX metadata integrity.

## Reasoning and Evidence

I performed a systematic audit of the `example_paper.bib` file provided in the paper's LaTeX source tarball. My focus was on ensuring that technical acronyms (especially LLM and RL), proper nouns, and specific model names are correctly case-protected to prevent BibTeX from lowercase-ing them in the final bibliography.

### Identified Issues

1.  **Lowercase Acronyms in Titles:**
    *   `chen2023huatuogpt`: "llms" -> `{LLM}s`
    *   `wang2025modifying`: "llm" -> `{LLM}`
    *   `betley2025emergent`: "LLMs" -> `{LLM}s`
    *   `slocum2025believe`: "LLMs" -> `{LLM}s`

2.  **Missing Case Protection for Model and Framework Names:**
    *   `chen2023huatuogpt`: "Huatuogpt-ii" -> `{H}uatuo{GPT}-ii`
    *   `ghandeharioun2024patchscopes`: "Patchscopes" -> `{P}atchscopes`

3.  **Missing Case Protection for Organizations and Venues:**
    *   `cheng2025domain`: "EMNLP 2025" -> `{EMNLP} 2025`
    *   Many entries: "arXiv" -> `{arXiv}` (e.g., `greenblatt2024alignment`, `betley2025emergent`)
    *   `macdiarmid2025natural`: "RL" -> `{RL}`

4.  **Inconsistent Journal and Booktitle Capitalization:**
    *   `betley2025emergent`: "arXiv preprint arXiv:..." - the word "preprint" is often capitalized in other entries but not here.
    *   `cheng2023adapting`: "The Twelfth International Conference on Learning Representations" (ICLR) is written out in full but without protection.

## Conclusion

The bibliography shows a high frequency of unprotected acronyms, particularly "LLM" and "llm", which are central to the paper's domain. Without curly-brace protection, these will be rendered as lowercase "llm" in many standard ICML bibliography styles. Systematic application of brace protection is essential for professional and accurate citation rendering.
