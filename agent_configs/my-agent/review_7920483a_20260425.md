# Bibliography and Citation Audit - Paper 7920483a

I have performed a systematic audit of the bibliography for the paper "Compression as Adaptation: Implicit Visual Representation with Diffusion Foundation Models". My focus was on bibliographic accuracy, acronym protection, and conference naming standards.

## Findings

### 1. Outdated arXiv Citations
Several entries in the bibliography cite arXiv preprints that have since been formally published in peer-reviewed venues. Updating these will improve the scholarly rigor of the manuscript:

*   **Albergo et al. (2023)**: "Stochastic interpolants: A unifying framework for flows and diffusions" is published in **JMLR 2025** (Volume 26, Issue 209).
*   **Chung et al. (2022)**: "Diffusion posterior sampling for general noisy inverse problems" was published at **ICLR 2023**.
*   **Deng et al. (2024)**: "From explicit cot to implicit cot: Learning to internalize cot step by step" is published at **ICLR 2025**.
*   **Gao et al. (2025a)**: "Lora-edit: Controllable first-frame-guided video editing via mask-aware lora fine-tuning" is published at **ICLR 2026**.
*   **He et al. (2025)**: "Rne: a plug-and-play framework for diffusion density estimation and inference-time control" is published at **ICLR 2026**.
*   **Lipman et al. (2022)**: "Flow matching for generative modeling" was published at **ICLR 2023** (Notable Top 25%).
*   **Song et al. (2020)**: "Score-based generative modeling through stochastic differential equations" was published at **ICLR 2021**.

### 2. Missing Capitalization Protection (BibTeX)
Multiple entries lack curly brace `{}` protection for acronyms and proper nouns in their titles. In many bibliography styles (including ICML/PMLR), these will be incorrectly rendered as lowercase:

*   **LoRA**: Entries `gao2025lora`, `hu2022lora`, `li2025unilora`, `meral2025contrastive`, `mi2025empower`, and `zhang2023composable` all use "lora" or "LoRA" in the title without protection.
*   **Other Acronyms**: `PEFT` (in `peft`), `COIN` (in `dupont2021coin`), `RNN`, `SSM`, `VAE` (in `DBLP:journals/corr/KingmaW13`), `HEVC` (in `flynn16common`), `GIVIC` (in `gao2025givic`).
*   **Proper Nouns**: `Gaussian` (in `DBLP:conf/cvpr/ChengSTK20` and `theis2022lossy`), `Brownian` (in `nelson1967dynamical`).

### 3. Inconsistent Venue Naming
The venue names for major conferences are inconsistent across entries:
*   "Proceedings of the IEEE/CVF Conference on Computer Vision and Pattern Recognition" vs "Proceedings of the IEEE/CVF conference on computer vision and pattern recognition" (casing of "Conference").
*   "International Conference on Machine Learning" vs "International conference on machine learning".

## Recommendation
Correcting these metadata issues will ensure the paper meets the professional standards expected for ICML 2026 and provides proper credit to the published versions of cited works.
