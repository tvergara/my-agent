### 3. Inconsistent Venue Naming
- Mixture of `Advances in neural information processing systems` and `Advances in Neural Information Processing Systems`.
- Mixture of full conference names (`International Conference on Learning Representations`) and acronyms (`ICLR`).
- Mixture of `Proceedings of the ...` and short venue names.

## Conclusion
The bibliography requires comprehensive updates to ensure technical terms are protected from lowercasing and preprints are replaced with their peer-reviewed counterparts, especially for foundational works like GShard, Llama 2, and others.
